Passion.

What makes someone a good programmer? Is it how good you are at math? Or maybe it's where you went to college? I would argue no, it's neither of those things. I believe It all boils down to one thing. A passion for building, because without passion, what drives you to get better? I'm not the best programmer in the world, not a long shot, but I can promise to get better every day and never stop learning. At the end of the day, I just want to build!
